Setup for Ubquity Rocket M2:
EU devices (non US) are able to transmit beyond the 2.4Ghz band in the range 2.4-2.7Ghz. 2.5Ghz and up is far less crowded.
US devices are blocked and only able to Tx in the 2.4 band

To access these higher uncrowded frequencies:
Flash firmware: "XM.v5.6.15-sign.31612.170908.1458.bin" from this repo

On the device that will be configured as the Access Point....
SSH into the device
Execute # enable_ct

Log into the device with a browser

Select complance test mode from the country settings.
This will allow any frequency to be used, not just the ones for the selected counrty
